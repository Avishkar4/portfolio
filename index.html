01 — About

The thesis before the model.

I'm Avishkar — a finance student building toward a career as an Equity Research Analyst. What pulls me toward research isn't the spreadsheet, it's the question behind it: why did this happen, and what does it tell us about what happens next.

Outside of research, I've run event operations for 50+ person competitions and helped build a gym-activewear brand from the ground up, which is where the curiosity about "why" first turned into the discipline of "show me the numbers."
